MY LIBRARY

Open index.html in a modern web browser to use your personal book catalogue.

Your books, wishlist, ratings, reading sessions, saved passages, compressed
photos, accounts, and community activity are stored locally in that browser.
No internet connection is needed.

On a phone, the photo controls can open the camera. On a computer, they open
the normal image picker.

Accounts are local to the browser and device. Passwords are stored as salted
hashes, but there is no online password recovery or cross-device sync.

The first account created in a browser is the administrator. Only that account
can see the Community admin controls and manage other local accounts.

Local users can follow one another, view profile reading statistics, and share
book recommendations and saved passages. Community activity is limited to
accounts created in the same browser on the same device.
